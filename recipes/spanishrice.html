Spanish Rice

Flavorful Spanish Rice and Beans

Very flavorful, quick, and simple rice and beans with a Mexican twist.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups of water

  • 1 cup uncooked white rice

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

  • 1 sweet onion, chopped

  • 1 (1.41 ounce) package sazon seasoning

  • 1 1/2 teaspoons adobo seasoning

  • 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic

  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

  • 1 (15 ounce) can red beans, drained

  • 1 (2,25 ounce) can pitted green olives (Optional)

Steps

  1. Bring water, rice, and olive oil to a boil in a saucepan over high heat.

  2. Mix in onion, sazon seasoning, adobo seasoning, garlic, and black pepper.

  3. Reduce heat to medium-low; cover and cook for 10 minutes.

  4. Add beans and olives and cook until liquid has been absorbed, about 10 minutes. Mix before serving
